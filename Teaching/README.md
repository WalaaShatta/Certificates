## What I Learned From This Course

* **Understanding how students learn:** Exploring different learning styles and how to adapt teaching methods to help every student understand.
* **Reflecting on teaching practices:** Developing the foundational skills needed for anyone starting their journey in education or instruction.
* **Structuring lesson goals:** Learning how to clearly define what students should get out of a session or a practical lesson.
* **Creating a supportive environment:** Methods for managing discussions, handling student questions, and building a positive learning space.
